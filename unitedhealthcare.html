Frequently asked questions

How can I prepare for my virtual visit?
The virtual visit checklist can help you get ready for a smooth visit.

Do I need to worry about the privacy of my health information?
All patient information will be kept secure and private as required by applicable laws. You can learn more about this on the virtual care providers’ websites.

Will I receive effective care through an online visit?
Yes! Virtual care providers are evaluated and chosen based on their ability to offer effective care online. If needed, your provider can guide you to in-person care for labs, specialists, imaging and more.

How long does it usually take to get seen by a provider?
You can receive same-day care with 24/7 Virtual Visits, and wait times are typically 15 minutes or less.

24/7 Virtual Visits is a service available with a provider via video, or audio-only where permitted under state law. It is not an insurance product or a health plan. Unless otherwise required, benefits are available only when services are delivered through a Designated Virtual Network Provider. 24/7 Virtual Visits are not intended to address emergency or lifethreatening medical conditions and should not be used in those circumstances.
